PREMIERE PRO

We got an assignment in school, where we had to film a day in our life. I had many ideas what to film, but I found it hard to film it. I’m not used to filming, I much rather wanna take pictures. I settled on filming me and my girlfriend making some Christmas food, so I drew a storyboard so that I had a plan on what to film and how. I tried to get some good close-up angles, but I found it difficult.

When I came to the edit part, I found it much more fun. It was very fun how a few cuts could make a video look so much different, and also putting filters and music on. I used one of the tones PremierePro offers, because I found it very Christmas like

I would love to challenge myself by doing more videos and try and make great edits. I can see myself be editing videos for influencers, as short videos are starting to pop up a lot, and would definitely be interesting in doing that, because it would challenge me and I would be learning a lot from it, plus it’s fun.
