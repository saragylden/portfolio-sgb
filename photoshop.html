PHOTO RETOUCH

In my spare time I like being creative and challenge myself. One of my favorite programs are Photoshop. I love to play around in the program and edit pictures. I edit some pictures for some fashion influencers, which they use on their page.

A fashion influencer named Sam (@__sampatrick) asked me if I could spice up his close-up sneaker picture and I thought it would be cool to add some neon around the shoe. I used Photoshop, where I used pen tool to draw around the areas where I wanted the neon, then I used the brush tool (hard brush) and in the end I used brush tool again but a soft brush which add the neon spread.

If you want to get a picture edited something like Sams or if you have other ideas, feel free to contact me!
